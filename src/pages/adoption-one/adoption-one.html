God's Family Testing 1

  • From the Bible

    The faith and love that spring from the hope stored up for you in Heaven and about which you have already heard in the true message of the Gospel. - Colossians 1:5

  • His Character

    God (the creator) loves you because He is love and made you in His image and likeness. You have immense value!

  • His Relationship

    He is a good father who made you to be His child.

  • His Nature

    He is God (Father, Jesus the son, and Holy Spirit), who live in a relationship of perfect love and made you to be included and share this with them.
