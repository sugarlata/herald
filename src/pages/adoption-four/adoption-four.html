Adopted

The Father is inviting you to trust Him and believe what He has done for you in Jesus.
  • From the Bible

    For He has rescued us from the dominion of darkness and brought us into the Kingdom of the Son He loves, in whom we have redemption, the forgiveness of sins. - Colossians 1:13,14

  • My Gift

    He will forgive you and make you clean.

  • My Adoption

    He will adopt you as His child so you will know Him and His love for you.

  • My New Nature

    He will make you a new creation with a righteous nature.

  • My New Relationship

    The Father, Jesus, and Holy Spirit will come to you and make their home with you.
