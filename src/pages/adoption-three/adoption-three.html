Rescued

  • From the Bible

    But now He has reconciled you by Christ's physical body through death to present you holy in His sight, without blemish and free from accusation. - Colossians 1:22

  • His Life

    Jesus is God who became human. Although He was God, He lived as a man, in a human body and He demonstrated a perfect life of love, truth, and power towrads others because of His close relationship with His Father.

  • His Sacrifice

    Jesus demonstrated the full extent of God's love when He took on our sinful (independent/broken/human) nature in himself when He died for us. Three days later, the Father raised Him to life.

  • His Victory

    Jesus now lives with his Father forever, in victory over the devil, sin, guilt, shame, and death.
