• From the Bible

    Once you were alienated from God and were enemies in your minds. - Colossians 1:21

  • Our Past

    A deception long ago caused the first people to choose distrust of God their Father, trusting their own judgement instead.

  • Our Legacy

    That choice (sin) made them orphans because it broke their perfect relationship with God.

  • Our State

    We inherited their mindset of being an orphan from God - not knowing what was possible. We show this by the choices we make.

  • Our Search

    As a result we search for self-worth, fulfillment, and security apart from God because we feel He is distant.
